Humaucracy Health

Humaucracy Health was built to be different from previous health care models in 3 important ways: our focus on the individual, our community-based approach, and our proactive model of health care.

Humaucracy Health currently provides Primary Care, working with your existing health care coverage. A Humaucracy Health Nurse will be happy to show you how you can blend our Primary Care into your existing coverages, using the nine domains.

Features include offline access, in app payments, push notifications, user login authentication.
